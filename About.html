About Alan Ho

I am a Computer Science Senior at Portland State University currently focusing on backend systems, AI, and geospatial database management. I am passionate about bridging the gap between low-level hardware efficiency and scalable software architecture. My current work involves developing a Carputer Driving Tracker, where I leverage Python and Docker Compose to orchestrate a containerized backend. I utilize PostgreSQL with PostGIS to handle complex spatial data and real-time trajectory tracking.

Beyond my work in Python and SQL, I maintain a strong foundation in C++, allowing me to understand low-level system performance and memory management.

I am currently a full-time student at PSU while maintaining part-time employment at Goodwill. This dual commitment has been instrumental in developing my professional communication skills and the disciplined time management required for high-level software development.
